An Evening of Fine Art & Philanthropy.

The Invitation

John Pellegrene cordially invites you to an evening of fine art & philanthropy featuring Father Jerome Tupa.

Original paintings by Father Jerome Tupa — Benedictine monk, priest and celebrated painter — offered alongside illuminated pages from the St. John's Bible, pottery by Richard Bresnahan, an original work by Pablo Picasso, and fine art from distinguished private collections. All proceeds benefit the enduring missions of St. John's Abbey & St. John's University.
